Success Stories

  1. Kalpana Chawla: Excelled in aerospace engineering and space exploration, becoming the first woman of Indian origin in space as an astronaut aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia.

  2. Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw: Pioneered in biotechnology and pharmaceuticals as the founder of Biocon Limited, a leading biopharmaceutical company in India.

  3. Mary Kom: Achieved unparalleled success in sports, particularly boxing, becoming a six-time world champion and inspiring women athletes across India.
